🔬 Research

My research sits at the intersection of exercise physiology and applied sports performance science. I am particularly interested in understanding how the body responds to training stress and how we can better monitor and optimise athlete readiness.

Exercise Physiology Training Monitoring & Evaluation Physical Fitness Assessment Energy Metabolism Warm-up Protocols Functional Movement Screening
