Boyi Zhang

PhD Student in Sport Science

Deutsche Sporthochschule Köln  ·  Shanghai Jiao Tong University

I am a sport scientist interested in how the human body responds and adapts to exercise training. My work focuses on exercise physiology, training load monitoring, and physical fitness assessment, with the goal of bridging laboratory insights and real-world athletic performance.

🔬 Research

My research sits at the intersection of exercise physiology and applied sports performance science. I am particularly interested in understanding how the body responds to training stress and how we can better monitor and optimise athlete readiness.

Exercise Physiology Training Monitoring & Evaluation Physical Fitness Assessment Energy Metabolism Warm-up Protocols Master Athletes Sedentary Behaviour

Active

Investigating severity, symptoms, and associations with quality of life and inflammatory markers in master athletes following COVID-19 infection.
